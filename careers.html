Work at Volley

The Volley office is located in the heart of the beautiful Hayes Valley neighborhood in San Francisco. Our hybrid work model allows the ~60% of our local employees to enjoy in-person time with colleagues a couple days a week, with flexibility as needed. The remainder are spread across the country (and world!) and visit several times a year to enjoy team events and bonding experiences.
‍
At Volley, we place a huge importance on work-life balance. We invest in meaningful perks & benefits, including:

Fully-covered medical,
dental and vision for employees and dependents
16 paid holidays
Lunch, commuter & professional development stipends
Matched 401k plan
Parental leave: 24 weeks at 100% pay for birthing parents, 12 weeks at 100% pay for non-birthing parents
4 weeks of PTO
(2 week minimum!)
